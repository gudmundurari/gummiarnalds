Agalma III

Ragnar Jónsson
Zofia Tomczyk
Guðmundur Arnalds


The third installment to the Agalma Improvisation Series, the album mixes cello by Ragnar Jónsson, found sounds and ambient stylings by Zoifa Tomczyk and real time sound processing by Guðmundur Arnalds.
Recorded at Mengi on the 9th of October 2019
released December 20, 2019
 
❧
 
Ragnar Jónsson - Cello
Zofia Tomczyk - Electronics
Guðmundur Arnalds - Electronics
 
Recording engineer - Örlygur Steinar Arnalds
Mixing and Mastering - Örlygur Steinar Arnalds & Guðmundur Arnalds
Artwork - Ragnar Jónsson

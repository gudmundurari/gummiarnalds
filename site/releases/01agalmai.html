Agalma I

Hilde Wollenstein
Ida Schuften Juhl
Guðmundur Arnalds


 
The first album in the "Agalma Improvisation Series".
The session was recorded in Mengi on the 23rd of July and features Hilde Wollenstein, Ida Schuften Juhl & Guðmundur Arnalds.
released October 9, 2019
 
❧
 
Hilde Wollenstein: Clarinet
Ida Schuften Juhl: Piano & Electronics
Guðmundur Arnalds: Electronics
 
Artwork: Jan van der Kleijn
Recording: Guðmundur Arnalds
Mixing and Mastering: Guðmundur Arnalds & Hilde Wollenstein
